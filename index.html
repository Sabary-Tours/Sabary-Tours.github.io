TAKE ME TO THE MOUNTAINS

Wli waterfall is the highest waterfall in West Africa and Ghana.Its located in the Agumatsa municipality located in the Volta Region of Ghana. It measures 143 metres from the Upper to the lower the plunge .Before getting to the waterfall you need to hike for about 30-40 minutes through an indigenous forest. The waterfall is in two parts which are the upper and the lower plunge .A tour guide is provided to enlighten you on the waterfall and a history of the township is provided .A tour to the Wli waterfall is definately worth taking and its location is perfect for cooling on
